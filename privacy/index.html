Privacy Policy

We collect what we need. Nothing more.

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Droptimize audits, rebuilds, and watches over websites. To do that we collect a small amount of data, we are upfront about who we share it with, and we never sell your data. Full stop.

1. Who we are

Droptimize is a product of OYE Creations LLC ("we", "us"). This policy covers droptimize.org and the Droptimize audit, rebuild, and Audit Watch monitoring services. If you have questions, reach us through the chat bubble on any Droptimize page.

2. What we collect

WhatWhy
Your email address and nameTo respond to audit requests, deliver reports, send receipts, and run your Audit Watch subscription. Only if you submit a form or buy.
The website URLs you submit to audit or to monitorThis is the core of the service. We fetch and analyze the sites you ask us to audit or watch.
Audit results (scores, headers, findings) for sites you submitTo show you the report and, for Audit Watch, to compare against future checks and alert you to changes.
Web push subscriptions, if you turn on alertsTo send Audit Watch notifications to your browser or installed app.
Payment detailsHandled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your full card number.
Basic technical and usage data (IP address, request counts)To prevent abuse and keep the service running. Not used to profile or advertise to you.

3. How monitoring works

When you add a site to Audit Watch, our monitoring system periodically fetches that site and re-runs the audit, then compares the new results to the previous ones. If scores, security headers, or uptime change in a way that matters, we send you an alert. We only fetch and analyze the sites you have asked us to watch, and only sites you have confirmed you are authorized to monitor.

4. Who we share data with

We use a small set of trusted service providers to run Droptimize. We share only what each one needs to do its job:

  • Google PageSpeed Insights - the URLs you submit are sent to Google's PageSpeed Insights API to generate the performance, SEO, accessibility, and best-practices scores that power our audits and monitoring.
  • Stripe - payment processing.
  • Cloudflare - hosting, security, and storage of your submissions, audit history, and subscription records.
  • Resend - transactional email (audit results, receipts, and account notices).

We do not sell your personal data, and we never will. We do not share it with advertisers or data brokers.

5. How long we keep it

We keep submission records and audit history for as long as we need them to run the service or your Audit Watch subscription, and to meet our legal and accounting obligations. Short-lived technical data, such as rate-limit counters, expires automatically. You can ask us to delete your data and close out your monitoring at any time.

6. Your rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct it if it is wrong.
  • Delete your data and cancel any monitoring.
  • Export the data you have given us.

To make any of these requests, contact us through the chat bubble on our site. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR (Europe) or the CCPA (California). We honor those rights for everyone.

7. Age and intended use

Droptimize is built for businesses and professionals and is intended for users aged 18 and older. It is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

8. Cookies

We use only the cookies and local storage needed to run the site and remember your preferences. We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies.

9. Changes to this policy

If we make a meaningful change, we will update the date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify account holders by email. Continued use of Droptimize after a change means you accept the updated policy.

10. Contact

Questions about your privacy or this policy? Reach us through the chat bubble on droptimize.org and we will get back to you.
